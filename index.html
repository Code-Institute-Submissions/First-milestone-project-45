Why live in an eco home?

Lower cost, affordable, mortgage free, better for the environment, off the grid living, ecologically sustainable...

Earthships

Built with natural, recycled materials, Earthships create comfortable and cosy living spaces without relying on public utilities. They're totally self-sufficient, producing their own electricity, heating and food, whilst managing sustainable water harvesting and sewage systems too.

Tiny Houses

At Tiny Eco Homes UK Ltd we specialise in custom built road legal tiny houses for customers across the UK. We do not just supply standard fully built tiny houses, 90% of our custom builds are fully bespoke and are designed by our clients to suit their needs. There are unlimited designs and options when building a tiny house, so let your imagination run wild as there is nothing we can not do

Shipping Container Homes

To some the thought of building a house out of steel shipping containers fills them with dread. However the real picture is somewhat different as both architects and self build home owners have proved that beautiful structures including houses can be built using this method. The flexibility of adding more space when needed, the cost savings from conventional building methods and the choice of finish makes the shipping container home a thing of beauty.
